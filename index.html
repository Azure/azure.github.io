Hello, cloud developers!

The Microsoft Azure teams are busy crafting frameworks and experiences to help make your projects successful.

We create SDKs that connect to Azure services through REST wrappers and interfaces in the languages of your choice including .NET, Java, Node.js, Python, Golang, Ruby and others.

We accept your open source contributions and license these works with Apache 2.0 or MIT Licenses.

Learn how to contribute

Open Source

Microsoft’s commitment to openness and collaboration is ingrained in our day-to-day approach to doing business with partners around the world.

This commitment influences how we’re helping customers transition to the cloud and make the most of IT trends that can boost flexibility, drive efficiency, and improve analytics.
